# Swap Thy Glory 👑🐀
**Game Jam Cyberiada 2026 Project by LEMTR/X**

## 🎮 About The Game
A 2.5D puzzle platformer where reality is split into CMYK layers. A cursed King must swap consciousness with his subjects (a Knight, a Cook, a Jester, and a Rat) to manipulate the environment, solve puzzles, and finally reach his tomb. Developed in Unreal Engine.

## 🛠️ My Contributions
Game jams are pure chaos, so everyone does a bit of everything. Here is what I specifically focused on during the jam:

* **Game & Puzzle Design:** Brainstormed and structured the core CMYK mind-swapping mechanics and the step-by-step puzzle logic (e.g., item combinations, NPC visibility rules).
* **Data Architecture (JSON):** Designed the JSON structures for dialogues, item interactions, and achievement systems, allowing the programmer to easily pull data without hardcoding text into Unreal Engine.
* **Documentation:** Co-authored and maintained the Game Design Document (GDD) to keep the team aligned on mechanics and level flow.
* **Writing:** Created the one-liners, item descriptions, and the overall slightly absurd, meta-lore of the cursed castle.
